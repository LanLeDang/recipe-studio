Classic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
