About Recipe Studio

Hello! Our names are Cristin Khor and Lan Le Dang, and we are both seniors studying Computer Science at Georgia State University. We created Recipe Studio as a cooking project because we wanted to share some of our favorite recipes in a way that feels organized, creative, and enjoyable to explore.

Recipe Studio was designed as a chapter-style recipe experience. The goal of the website is to present cooking content in a clear, structured, and visually appealing way while also demonstrating strong HTML and CSS design practices. Rather than placing every recipe in one long list, the site is organized so users can move through each section in a natural and engaging order.

Each section of the website represents a different part of a meal, allowing users to explore recipes in a logical sequence from appetizers to desserts. This layout helps keep the content organized, easy to follow, and more interactive for the user. Above, we included some of our favorite recipes in hopes of sharing these amazing dishes with others.
